个人简历：
Jenny joined Shell Technology Center in Houston in 1900, right after received her MS in Chemical Engineering from University of Texas at Austin in U.S.
Jenny has over 25 years of experience with both technical and business development in the downstream and upstream sectors. She has worked as a global business manager, principal advisor, project lead, senior consultant, and R&D researcher within Shell. She also holds US patent & patent disclosures.

Since 2012, Jenny moved to Shell (China) in Beijing, she has led the Technical & New Business Development group. Her professional focus is to understand clients’ needs/challenges, and to develop optimal technical proposals for potential coal gasification projects.
Jenny also represented a Shell’s recruiting team at University of Texas in Austin, and performed campus interview & conducted corporate engagement with students from Engineering College at UT.
